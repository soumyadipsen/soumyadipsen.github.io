DISCOVER THE BRAND NEW SAMZWORLD SERIES.

Exterior Design.
Powerful lines and large inlets indicate just how much energy is at your disposal in the Samzworld Series. Flat, focused headlights, which are available as an option with full LED technology, point towards the heart of the front – the double kidney grille with a chrome surround. The dynamic overall impression whets the appetite for unadulterated driving sensations. At the rear, large wheel housings and a wide track emphasise the close contact with the road surface. The L-shaped LED rear lights are an undeniable eye-catcher and demonstrate immediately that it’s a typical Samzworld that’s conquering the streets.




Interior Design.
Fresh, modern and straightforward – that’s the impression created by the interior of the Samzworld Series at first glance. And then confirmed at second glance. The cockpit has typical Samzworld driver orientation and the whole interior is characterised by top-quality design right down to the last detail.
